About Me

Hello! I'm Sarah Maslin-Bosher, a passionate photographer and videographer based in London. With a keen eye for detail and a love for visual storytelling, I specialize in capturing moments that tell compelling stories.

My work spans across various genres including portrait photography, event coverage, commercial projects, and creative videography. I believe that every frame should evoke emotion and convey a unique narrative.

Whether it's documenting life's precious moments or creating content for brands, I approach each project with creativity, professionalism, and dedication to delivering exceptional results.
